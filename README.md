# 📊 Vrinda Store Annual Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard developed to analyze the annual sales performance of Vrinda Store for the year 2022. The dashboard helps identify customer behavior, sales trends, top-performing states, sales channels, and product categories to support business decision-making and increase future sales.

## Business Objective

Vrinda Store wants to create an annual sales report for 2022 to better understand its customers and sales performance. The insights derived from the dashboard can help the business improve marketing strategies and increase sales in future years.

## Key Business Questions

* Compare sales and orders using a single chart.
* Which month achieved the highest sales and orders?
* Who purchased more products: Men or Women?
* What are the different order statuses in 2022?
* Which states contributed the highest sales?
* What is the relationship between age and gender based on sales?
* Which sales channel generated the highest revenue?
* Which product category achieved the highest sales?
